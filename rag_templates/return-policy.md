# Return & Refund Policy

## Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we're here to help.

## Return Eligibility

### Digital Products / Subscriptions
- **Refund Window:** [14/30] days from purchase date
- **Conditions:** If you haven't used the service extensively (less than [X]% of included resources consumed)
- **Pro-rated Refunds:** Available for annual plans cancelled mid-term

### Physical Products
- **Return Window:** [30] days from delivery date
- **Condition:** Item must be unused and in original packaging
- **Exceptions:** Customized or personalized items cannot be returned

## How to Request a Return or Refund

1. **Contact Support:** Email refunds@yourcompany.com or use the live chat
2. **Provide Details:** Include your order number, purchase date, and reason for return
3. **Await Confirmation:** We'll review your request within [1-2] business days
4. **Process:** Follow the instructions provided (for physical items, a prepaid return label will be sent)

## Refund Processing

- **Credit Card:** Refund appears within 5-10 business days
- **PayPal:** Refund appears within 3-5 business days
- **Bank Transfer:** Refund appears within 5-7 business days
- **Store Credit:** Issued immediately

## Cancellation Policy

### Subscription Cancellations
- Cancel anytime from Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription
- Access continues until the end of your current billing period
- No partial-month refunds for monthly plans
- Annual plans: pro-rated refund for remaining full months

### Service Cancellations (Appointments/Bookings)
- **More than 24 hours before:** Full refund
- **12-24 hours before:** 50% refund
- **Less than 12 hours before:** No refund
- **No-show:** No refund

## Exchanges

We offer free exchanges for physical products within the return window. Contact support to arrange an exchange.

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:
1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
2. Include photos of the damage
3. We'll ship a replacement at no cost or issue a full refund
