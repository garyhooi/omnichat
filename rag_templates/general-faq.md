# General FAQ

## Account & Access

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the link in the reset email. The link expires after 1 hour.

### How do I update my profile information?
Go to Settings > Profile. You can update your name, email, phone number, and profile picture.

### Can I have multiple accounts?
Each email address can only be associated with one account. If you need multiple workspaces, use our team/organization feature instead.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted within 30 days per our data retention policy.

## Billing & Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers (for Enterprise plans).

### How do I update my payment method?
Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Method. You can add, remove, or update your card details at any time.

### Where can I find my invoices?
All invoices are available under Settings > Billing > Invoice History. You can download them as PDF.

### Will I be charged automatically?
Yes, subscriptions renew automatically. You'll receive an email reminder 7 days before your renewal date.
